Honey
Garlic Chicken

Prep: 4 mins

Cook: 8 mins

Total: 12 mins

Servings:2

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken breast , boneless and skinless (2 pieces)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1/4 of flour
  • 3.5 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1.5 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of honey

    • Directions

    2. Dust chicken with flour
    3. Sear chicken, add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey.
    4. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Turn chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce.
