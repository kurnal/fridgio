Creamy
Mushroom Soup

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 1 hr 20 mins

Total: 1 hr 35 mins

Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • 2 pounds sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream

    • Directions

    1. Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium-high heat
    2. Cook mushrooms in butter with 1 pinch salt until the mushrooms give off their juices; reduce heat to low.
    3. Continue to cook, stirring often, until juices evaporate and the mushrooms are golden brown, about 15 minutes. Set aside a few attractive mushroom slices for garnish later, if desired.
    4. Mix onion into mushrooms and cook until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 more minutes.
    5. Stir flour into mushroom mixture and cook, stirring often, for 2 minutes to remove raw flour taste.
    6. Add garlic cloves. Pour chicken stock and water into mushroom mixture.
    7. Bring to simmer and cook for 1 hour.
    8. Stir in cream. Season with salt and peppper and serve in bowls with mushroom slices.
